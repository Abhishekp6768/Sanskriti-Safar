About SanskritiSafar

Discover India With Us

SanskritiSafar is a premier travel company specializing in cultural and heritage tours across India. Founded in 2010, we have been helping travelers experience the rich diversity of Indian culture, history, and traditions.

Our mission is to provide authentic, immersive travel experiences that go beyond typical tourist attractions. We work with local communities to create sustainable tourism opportunities that benefit both travelers and residents.

Our Journey

SanskritiSafar was founded by a group of travel enthusiasts who wanted to showcase India's cultural heritage in an authentic way. What started as a small operation with just three destinations has now grown into one of India's leading cultural tour operators.

We pride ourselves on our knowledgeable guides, carefully curated itineraries, and commitment to responsible tourism. Our team includes historians, anthropologists, and local experts who ensure every tour is both educational and enjoyable.
